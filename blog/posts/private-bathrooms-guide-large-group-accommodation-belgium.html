Understanding Bathroom Configurations

When searching for large group accommodation Belgium, it's important to understand different bathroom configurations:

Multiple bedrooms share one or more bathrooms, typically located in hallways or common areas. Common ratios:

  • Acceptable: 4-5 people per bathroom (still creates delays)
  • Better: 2-3 people per bathroom (but still shared)

For groups of 30-40 guests, shared bathrooms almost always create inconvenience, especially during peak usage times.

En-Suite/Private Bathrooms (Ideal)

Each bedroom has its own private bathroom. This means:
